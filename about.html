Our Story

About Dhanu Computers

Dhanu Computers began in 2013 with a simple belief: technology should serve people clearly, not confuse them. What started as a small service center in Bengaluru has grown into a trusted resource for individuals and businesses who value reliability over hype.

We don't chase trends. We don't oversell. We guide people toward systems that fit their actual work — whether that's a refurbished laptop for a student, a precision repair for a professional, or a long-term relationship with a growing business.

"We believe technology should feel aligned — not confusing, not excessive, not wrong for your purpose."

Over the years, we've restored hundreds of systems, advised countless buyers, and built relationships that extend beyond single transactions. Our reputation is word-of-mouth driven, because the work speaks for itself.

Most of our clients return. Some for upgrades. Some for repairs. Some because they trust our advice when making their next decision. That continuity is what defines us.

The Journey

2013

Established in Bengaluru with a focus on honest laptop repair and sales. No false promises, no unnecessary upselling — just clear technical service.

2015-2017

Expanded into refurbished laptop sales as clients sought reliable, affordable alternatives. Built relationships with corporate clients seeking bulk support.

2018-2020

Refined precision repair capabilities — chip-level diagnostics, motherboard repair, data integrity. Reputation grew through referrals, not advertising.

2021-2023

Established as a trusted name among students, professionals, and local businesses. Focused on long-term client relationships and sustained service quality.

2024-Present

Continuing with the same principle: technology chosen with clarity. Serving Bengaluru with integrity, technical expertise, and genuine guidance.
